Exploring the Roots of Chronic Pain: Socioeconomic & Environmental Insights

Master’s Capstone • University at Albany (SUNY) • Dataset: World Health Survey (n = 54,019)

Project Overview

Chronic pain undermines well-being and productivity globally. This project examines how GDP per capita, civil conflict, education, age, and gender influence self-reported pain levels using regression and interaction models.
